What's a Coinhako Privé member?

Individuals and businesses that are categorised as accredited investors (AI) can look to come onboard as a Coinhako Privé customer and enjoy our world-class digital asset services.

Before we embark on this esteemed business relationship, please take your time to understand the requirements to qualify as an AI.

High-net worth individuals

Annual income exceeding S$300k

OR Financial assets (net of any related liabilities) exceeding S$1 million in value

OR Net personal asses exceeding S$2 million in value

Institutions / corporates:

Net assets exceeding S$10 million in value (or its equivalent in foreign currency)
