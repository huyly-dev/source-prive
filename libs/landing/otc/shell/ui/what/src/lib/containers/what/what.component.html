What is crypto
OTC trading?

Over-the-counter trading, or OTC trading, refers to trades that are not made on a formal exchange.

In the context of cryptocurrencies, OTC trades are usually private deals made between two parties to trade cryptocurrencies. This would involve one party who is willing to sell their crypto assets at a given price and another willing to buy certain crypto assets at a given price. An OTC trading deal is made when both parties reach a consensus on the pricing that they want for the trade and exchange of the assets is settled privately between both parties.

OTC trading deals can also be done as a fiat-to-crypto exchange, crypto-to-fiat exchange, or even a crypto-to-crypto exchange.
